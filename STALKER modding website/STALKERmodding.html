Tutorials, modding guides and more

Monolith modding is a website for all the modding enthusiasts in the STALKER community. It contains extensive guides for many topics helping YOU bring your imagination to life and make your first S.T.A.L.K.E.R. mods. All the guides are free to use and replicate, and discussing the mods in the @discussion page is highly encouraged. Happy modding!
